It presents a comparison in the principal component analysis (PCA)
space between the ground-truth numerical solution and the predicted solutions
obtained by the U2E-evolved method and the DIMON method after learning
the reaction–diffusion dynamics on a parametrized two-dimensional elliptical
domain. The horizontal and vertical axes correspond to the first two principal
components (PC1 and PC2), respectively. Blue dots represent the ground-truth
solutions, while red dots denote the model-predicted solutions.
